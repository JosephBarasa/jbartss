African Grace II

Medium : Oil on Glass Bottle
Year : 2023
Kshs.700


This artwork serves as a testament to the artist's deep connection
with the subject matter and their commitment to portraying the strength,
beauty, and resilience of African women
through the universal language of art.
Spectrum of Emotions

Medium : Oil on Glass Bottle
Year : 2023
Kshs.700


At first glance, the piece may appear chaotic and fragmented,
but upon closer inspection, intricate details emerge,
revealing a multitude of emotions woven together in a harmonious dance.
The artist's brushstrokes are energetic and expressive,
capturing the raw intensity of emotions ranging from joy and passion to sorrow and contemplation.
